Pet Grooming

We love to groom pets. We know that not everyone does, but we’re pretty sure that everyone loves a clean one.

That's why House Pets Spa is here to help. We’ll always listen to what you need, whether that's a hygienic pet trim or breed standard cut. We can offer advice on how to achieve the best results and you will always receive 100% honesty. We treat every pet as though it was our own - with kindness respect and patience.

Hygienic Care & Style

Grooming is a vital part of the well-being and happiness of your pet and that's our specialty. We are an Eco friendly modern salon that is open plan cage free facility, focusing on providing a clean and safe environment. House Pets Spa offer a range of grooming services to meet your needs. With personal care given to each pet to ensure their safety, happiness and to ensure a good service is given.

Caring for your pets coat will ensure health changes are noticed promptly, hygiene managed and shedding is minimized. We cater for all breeds of Dogs and Cats and can accommodate specific requests in regard to styling or dealing with medical conditions. We have availablity to accomodate sedation grooming in tamdum with Corby Veterinary Surgery should you require this service
